About Us

A workshop you can actually trust.

Automotive Solutions is a family-run mechanical workshop in the heart of Māori Hill, owned and run by Greg.

We've built our name on doing things the right way — honest advice, fair pricing, and quality workmanship on every job. No upselling, no surprises: just straight answers and reliable repairs that keep Dunedin families on the road.

We're proud to be part of the local community too — supporting local kids' sports teams and the people who keep Māori Hill ticking.
